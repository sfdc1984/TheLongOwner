DriveLab Conclusion
Short coastal drives are where fuel economy swings the most — 24.2 km/l in daylight on the open ECR fell to 20.3 km/l after dark with lights, AC and slower village crossings added in. The car doesn't have one economy figure; it has a range shaped by the road.
